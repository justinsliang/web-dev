About Us

We have a very humble beginning. Our company started when a person on the streets was caring for multiple cats at a time. We realized that he had a kind heart and was using it to take care of other animals lives. We were inspired by this man and decided to build a cat cafe to welcome people and cats at the same time. We adopted cats and also took cats from the streets to give them a better life. We first had the funds to start our business when we requested a donation from Elon Musk, who happily agreed and helped us off our feet. We adopted our first cats to see how to take care of them. When we were used to taking care of adopted cats, we went onto the streets to take in cats that had nowhere to go. The cats were a bit hostile at first but became comfortable with their new surroundings. We went through a lot of hard work to become this magnificent restaraunt that we are today. Hope you enjoy!!!
